Research Vision and Mission

The primary mission of the Applied Mathematics and Data Science Research Group (AMaDaS) is to advance rigorous and impactful research at the intersection of applied mathematics and data-driven methodologies.

  • Develop mathematically grounded models for modern data analysis problems
  • Bridge theoretical mathematics with data science, machine learning, and AI
  • Promote reproducible, interpretable, and reliable computational methods

The group places strong emphasis on model interpretability, robustness, and generalization, ensuring that research outcomes are not only performant but also scientifically meaningful.
