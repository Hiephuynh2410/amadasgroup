Applied Mathematics and Data Science Research Group

The Applied Mathematics and Data Science (AMaDaS) Research Group is a research unit at Tien Giang University dedicated to applied mathematics, data science, and artificial intelligence. The group focuses on developing advanced mathematical models, algorithms, and data-driven methodologies that bridge fundamental research with real-world applications in education, healthcare, economics, agriculture, and digital transformation. Through its research activities, the AMaDaS Group aims to produce high-quality international publications, promote knowledge and technology transfer, and foster an open academic environment for training and mentoring researchers, graduate students, and undergraduates.
